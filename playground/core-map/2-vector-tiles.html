2. Vector tiles

Zero-dep MVT pipeline. Fetches .pbf tiles, decodes them through the in-house protobuf reader, and rasterizes with Canvas2D. Style layers declare the source layer, the draw type (fill / line / circle), and the paint properties. Click anywhere on the map to queryRenderedFeatures and inspect the hit features below.

Synthetic tile source. Keyless MVT basemaps on the public internet are unreliable, so this demo generates a single .pbf tile in the browser and intercepts fetch for the demo-tiles/ path. To point at a real tile server, reload with ?tiles=https://your/{z}/{x}/{y}.pbf in the URL.
